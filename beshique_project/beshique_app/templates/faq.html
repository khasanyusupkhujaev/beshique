Frequently Asked Questions

What is Beshique?

Beshique is a smart baby cradle that automatically detects your baby’s wakefulness and gently rocks them back to sleep using adaptive motion and calming sounds.

How does the cradle know when to start rocking?

Beshique uses built-in computer vision and audio sensors to detect signs of wakefulness — such as eye movement, motion, and sound — and responds with appropriate rocking intensity based on your baby’s state.

Is it safe for newborns?

Yes. Beshique follows global safety standards and is designed for infants from 0 to 24 months. The rocking motion is gentle and tested extensively to ensure it's soothing and secure.

Can I control or customize the cradle?

Absolutely. The cradle connects to a mobile app where you can view sleep analytics, adjust motion levels, and change sound settings. It also works automatically out of the box with no setup required.

What if my baby wakes up crying?

If Beshique detects crying, it will automatically switch to a higher bounce level and play soothing audio. If the baby doesn’t calm within a few minutes, you’ll receive an alert through the app.

Does Beshique work without the app?

Yes, the cradle works independently with its built-in intelligence. The app is optional for monitoring and customization.

What sounds does Beshique play?

It comes with a selection of white noise, lullabies, womb-like sounds, and nature audio — all chosen for their proven calming effect on babies.

How do I clean the cradle?

The mattress and mesh sides are removable and machine-washable. The body can be wiped down with a damp cloth. We’ve designed everything for quick and easy maintenance.
